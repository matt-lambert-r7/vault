### Friction
- Still not 100% clear how this terminology maps to the 90 day work we have done already. It feels like it might not be consistent
- I understand the need for a solid page template system. It's a must. But its also a double edge sword. As I read some of these docs I wonder if they are over prescribing the solution. What if something doesn't fit into this pattern... eg: chatUI... What if not allowing wider layout exploration risks better solutions not being found?
- When it comes to configurable layout frameworks, I think there may be a middle step before you go custom layout. That being, progressive disclosure based on the persona or role. Often being more opinionated, based on user role, will meet the goals of the majority of users. Deeper customization can be considered after that point
- When you start to get into customization, there is also another customer ask. This is why I tend to lean in the headless direction for those users. Let them figure it out while we design for the majority of users who want a similar experience

### Ideas
- Encourage exploration in layout and interaction patterns. But, require new patterns to be approved and built into the larger system
- Instead of investing the effort into a customizable UI, has a headless option been considered with a sample app out of the box. The user can then use the sample app for reference, customizing it, or throwing it away and building from scratch
